About Us

Who We Are

Our team, comprising Muhammad Haashid, Mohammed Shakeeb, Fathimath Ansheera, and Mohannad Kallumanti, is dedicated to exploring new approaches to solve complex challenges. Through our project, BioFlow, we aim to make a positive impact by transforming fish waste into valuable resources, contributing to a sustainable future. BioFlow directly connects businesses (B2B) with no middlemen, streamlining the process for maximum efficiency. BioFlow's key features include direct B2B connection, waste transformation, sustainable impact, and efficiency.

Our Mission

Our mission at BioFlow is to revolutionize the fishing industry by transforming fish waste into valuable resources. We aim to connect businesses with excess fish waste to those in need of sustainable resources, thereby reducing waste and fostering a circular economy. We are also hiring freelance truck drivers for the transportation of waste, ensuring efficient and timely delivery. By providing a cost-effective and eco-friendly solution, we strive to positively impact industries and communities, promoting responsible consumption and environmental stewardship. Join us in reshaping the future of resource management and sustainability.
